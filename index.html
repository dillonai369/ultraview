The Ultra View Difference

A 30-foot pole that reaches what ladders can't

Most streaks come from soap and tap-water minerals left drying on the glass. We skip both. Our water-fed pole runs purified, deionized water through a soft brush — it scrubs the dirt loose and rinses clean, then dries spot-free on its own.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How much does window cleaning cost?

It's simple: $5 per window and $3 per screen for an exterior, purified-water clean. Count your windows and you'll know the price before we ever show up. Get 3 or more homes on your block to book together and everyone takes 10% off.

Do you clean second-story windows?

Yes. Our 30-foot water-fed pole safely reaches the windows on a two-story home from the ground — no ladders leaning against your house, no risk to your siding or landscaping.

Why are your windows streak-free?

We use purified, deionized water instead of soap and tap water. Tap-water minerals are what leave spots when they dry — pure water rinses clean and dries on its own with no streaks.

What towns do you serve?

We focus on La Grange, La Grange Park, Western Springs and Hinsdale, plus nearby western suburbs like Brookfield, Clarendon Hills and Burr Ridge. Just outside the area? Ask — we're expanding.

Do you really clean trash cans too?

We do. We deep-clean, deodorize and sanitize the inside of garbage and recycling bins so trash day doesn't smell like trash day. Ask about one-time and recurring pricing.
