What Does Window Cleaning Cost in La Grange & the Western Suburbs?

By Ultra View · June 17, 2026 · 5 min read

Most window cleaning websites make you "call for a quote" before they'll tell you a single number. We think that's annoying, so here's the straight talk on what window cleaning actually costs in La Grange, Western Springs, Hinsdale and the rest of the western suburbs — and exactly what makes your price go up or down.

Quick answer: Ultra View charges $5 per window and $3 per screen. A typical home lands between $80 and $200 depending on how many windows and screens you have — and blocks that book together save 10%.

How window cleaning gets priced

Companies price one of three ways: per window, per hour, or a flat per-visit rate. We price per window because it's the most honest — you can count your own windows and know roughly what you'll pay before we ever pull up. No mystery, no padded hourly clock.

What affects your total

  • Number of windows. The biggest factor, and the easiest to estimate: $5 each.
  • Screens. Clean glass behind a dusty screen still looks dirty. Screens are $3 each and make the whole window look new.
  • Stories. We reach every second-story window — and even some third stories — from the ground with our water-fed pole, so height doesn't blow up your bill the way ladder work does.
  • First clean vs. maintenance. If it's been a few years and there are baked-on hard-water spots, the first visit can take longer. After that, regular cleanings are quick.

A real example

Say you've got a two-story home with 20 windows and 15 screens:

  • 20 windows × $5 = $100
  • 15 screens × $3 = $45
  • Total: $145 — and about $130 if you and a couple neighbors book together.

That's it. No trip fee, no "fuel surcharge," no surprise line items.

The neighborhood deal

Because we work whole blocks at a time, we pass the efficiency back to you: get 3 or more homes on your street to book the same day and everyone gets 10% off. It's the easiest way to lower your price — just tell a couple neighbors.

Is professional cleaning worth it?

For the price of a nice dinner out, you skip the ladder, the streaks, and an afternoon of frustration — and you get a better result because we use purified water that dries with zero streaks. Want your exact number? Grab a free quote and we'll count them up with you.

Frequently asked questions

How much does window cleaning cost in La Grange?

Ultra View charges $5 per window and $3 per screen. Most homes fall between $80 and $200 depending on window and screen count, and blocks that book together save 10%.

Do you charge extra for second-story windows?

No. Our 30-foot water-fed pole reaches second-story — and even some third-story — windows from the ground, so it's the same $5 per window.

No. We price per window and screen with no trip fees or surcharges. Contact us and we'll give you a clear total up front.
