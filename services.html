Service 01

Exterior Window Cleaning

The job homeowners dread, done in a fraction of the time and twice as well. We use a 30-foot water-fed pole that runs purified, deionized water through a soft brush — scrubbing dirt loose and rinsing it clean. The water dries on its own with zero spots or streaks, and we never lean a ladder against your house.

  • $5 per window — clear, per-window pricing
  • Reaches second-story and even some third-story windows from the ground
  • Purified water = no soap, no minerals, no streaks

Service 02

Screen Cleaning

Clean glass behind a dusty screen still looks dirty. For just $3 a screen we clean the screens too, clearing out the pollen, cobwebs and grime so the whole window — glass and frame — looks brand new. It's the easiest add-on to a window visit.

  • Just $3 per screen
  • Clears pollen, dust and cobwebs
  • Improves airflow and the whole window's look

Service 03

Trash & Recycling Can Cleaning

Garbage and recycling bins build up grime, bacteria and a smell that lingers all week. We deep-clean the inside of your cans, then deodorize and sanitize so they're fresh again — no more holding your breath on trash day. Ask about one-time cleans and recurring plans to keep them fresh year-round.

  • Deep-cleaned, deodorized and sanitized inside & out
  • Kills the smell and bacteria at the bottom of the bin
  • One-time or recurring — your choice
