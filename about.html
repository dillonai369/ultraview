Our Story

Started local, built on doing it right

Ultra View was started by two hardworking young guys from the western suburbs — Tony and Eddie — who got tired of seeing dirty windows and dreaded chores piling up on busy families. So they invested in the right gear, learned the craft, and built a business on a simple promise: show up on time, charge a fair price, and leave every pane spotless.

Today we clean windows and trash cans all over La Grange, La Grange Park, Western Springs and Hinsdale — one block at a time. We're young, but we're serious about the work, and we'd rather earn a customer for life than rush a job.
